## Description

This game aims to evoke a meditative experience, allowing players to relax through calm and peaceful exploration. The camera automatically tracks a smoothly flying airplane, endlessly flying to the right. A deep parallax system creates a 3D illusion through three layers of twinkling star. As the airplane flies, birds will spawn randomly.
---

## Setup and Interaction Instructions

Sit back and observe. The experience is fully autonomous. Watch the parallax stars and look closely as the plane's presence reveals hidden flocks of birds in the darkness.

---

## Iteration Notes

a.Post-Playtest:

- Initially, the hidden objects were geometric runes. I replaced with procedurally animated birds.
- The initial bird design featured a glowing blue aura and tail. I removed the tail and aura, ultimately arriving at a cleaner, minimalist design.
- The original canvas was a standard white webpage. To create a “museum-like” display effect, the canvas was changed to pure black and centered.

b.Post-Showcase:

- The airplane can be controlled with the mouse, transforming the project from a passive environmental display into an active exploration game that allows players to actively seek out hidden birds.
- Background music and soundtracks can be added.
